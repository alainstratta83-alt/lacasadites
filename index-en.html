The Strategic Location

Ideal Altitude

Donnas is located at 322 meters altitude, at the entrance point of Aosta Valley. La Casa di Tes is situated on the Donnas hill at 550 meters.

Exceptional Climate

The privileged position ensures an exceptionally mild climate compared to the rest of the Alpine region, making it ideal in every season.

Donnas: A Village to Discover

Unique Historical Heritage

üèõÔ∏è The Arch and Roman Road of the Gauls

A masterpiece of engineering from the 1st century BC, when the Romans literally carved into the rock the consular road connecting Rome to the Rhone Valley. Walking under this arch means retracing the steps of history.

Land of Wine and Heroic Viticulture

Donnas is the cradle of Aosta Valley's first DOC wine, a full-bodied and prestigious red wine that bears the name of the village itself. The vineyards that climb mountain slopes are an example of heroic viticulture, where every grape is the result of centuries-old passion and effort.

The Castles of Aosta Valley

A journey through medieval towers, fortresses and royal residences in the heart of the Alps

Aosta Valley preserves over 70 castles and fortifications, testimony to a glorious past. Each manor tells stories of knights, noble families and epic battles, immersed in breathtaking Alpine landscapes.

F√©nis Castle

20 km from La Casa di Tes (~25 minutes)

The most famous Aosta Valley castle, with its crenellated towers and 15th-century frescoes. A masterpiece of perfectly preserved medieval architecture.

Savoy Castle - Gressoney

50 km from La Casa di Tes (~60 minutes)

The summer residence of Queen Margherita of Savoy, a jewel in eclectic style surrounded by Monte Rosa peaks. A fairytale castle in the heart of the Alps.

Panoramic Savoy Castle

50 km from La Casa di Tes (~60 minutes)

Panoramic view of Savoy Castle immersed in Alpine nature. Its five towers stand out against the sky of the Aosta Valley mountains.

Issogne Castle

8 km from La Casa di Tes (~12 minutes)

Elegant Renaissance residence with the famous porticoed courtyard and the "Pomegranate Fountain". A dive into 15th-century court life.

Verr√®s Castle

12 km from La Casa di Tes (~18 minutes)

Impressive military fortress with square plan, unique example of 14th-century monoblock architecture. Majestically dominates the valley below.

Fort of Bard

6 km from La Casa di Tes (~9 minutes)

Spectacular 19th-century military fortress that resisted Napoleon's siege. Today it hosts museums and modern art exhibitions.

Sarriod de la Tour Castle

30 km from La Casa di Tes (~35 minutes)

Elegant medieval castle in Saint-Pierre, famous for the "Hall of Heads" with 171 carved brackets. A treasure of Aosta Valley medieval art.
